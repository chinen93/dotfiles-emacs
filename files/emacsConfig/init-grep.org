** Search Home
:PROPERTIES:
:ID:       aea2c70b-59ff-4b5d-9019-03241295532c
:END:
#+BEGIN_SRC emacs-lisp
  (rg-define-search my-search-home
    "Search files including hidden in home directory"
    :query ask
    :format regexp
    :files "all"
    :flags ("--hidden --ignore-case")
    :dir (getenv "HOME")
    :menu ("Search" "h" "Home"))
#+END_SRC

:PROPERTIES:
